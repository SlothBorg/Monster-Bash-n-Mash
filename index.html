How? aka The Plan

The basic plan has four phases

  1. Create inspiration cards:

    I invite you all to get some paper, and draw an image, or write down a word/phrase to serve as inspiration, these can be silly, strange, scary, sexy, or some other word starting with s.

    For example: pictures of, bones, horns, scales, a tail, a mouth etc. or perhaps words/phrases like. Bone, Gnarly, bulbous, oozing, shimmering, pulsating pustules.

    Ideally this part would be completed before the party weekend, that way I can make a virtual deck of the cars for those not able to attend.

  2. Drawing Cars: aka determining your fate

    Each person draws some number, that number will be decide as a group, though you are welcome to draw more cards! Each card can be interpreted how ever you see fit.

  3. MAKE MONSTERS: aka The Really Fun Part!

    Using the cards you drew, make something!, It could be a sculpture, a miniature, a mosaic, collage, painting, poem, a program, website a short story or what ever else you feel moved to create!

  4. Show and Tell:

    Share what you made, take pictures of it, talk about it, etc.
